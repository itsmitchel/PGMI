About Us

Learn more about our church and community

Our Vision

Province Of Grace is committed to serving the community and spreading the message of faith, hope, and love. Founded in 2004, our church has grown into a vibrant community of believers dedicated to making a positive impact in our world.

Our mission is to equip people to live out their faith, build strong families, and reach out to those in need. We believe in the power of community and the strength that comes from worshiping and growing together.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, you'll find a warm welcome at PGMI Church.
